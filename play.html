How to Play

Noodle

Eat the meatballs to grow your snake

Try to fill the entire board with your snake to win.

If your snake's head hits the wall, you lose.

If your snake's head hits itself, you lose.

Candy Finger

Try to align 3 of the same letter in a row

X or O

If this cannot happen, it will result in a tie.

This is a two player game.

1024

Match 2 of the same number to double it

Your goal is to reach the 1024 tile

You can go for as long as you want

If the entire grid fills up with numbers, you lose.
